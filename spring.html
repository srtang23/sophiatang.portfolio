CONTEXT

Spring is a 3-day Springtime Picnic Festival for people who want to celebrate the season of spring and connect with loved ones. It features a mix of spring-themed activities and offers a unique way to enjoy the outdoors, indulge in the season's flavors, and create lasting memories with family and friends.

RESEARCH

Competitive Analysis

Analyzed three Seattle-area festivals to identify key success factors and opportunities:

Community & Cultural Engagement

Strong community ties and cultural programming drive attendance and create memorable experiences.

Diverse Audience Appeal

These festivals successfully attract families, tourists, and locals by offering varied activities and experiences.

Local Products & Crafts

Emphasis on local vendors supports the regional economy while promoting specialty products to a broader audience.

User Personas

Created personas to understand the diverse attendee types and their unique needs.

Scott - the Analytic Brewer

Age: 28

Tech-savvy professional who values data-driven decisions and enjoys craft beverages. Seeks authentic experiences and appreciates detailed event information.

Jocelyn - the International Student

Age: 22

Exploring local culture and seeking social connections. Budget-conscious and interested in unique experiences that help her connect with the community.

Grace - the ECO Mom

Age: 35

Family-oriented and environmentally conscious. Looks for kid-friendly activities and supports local, sustainable businesses. Values convenience and organization.
